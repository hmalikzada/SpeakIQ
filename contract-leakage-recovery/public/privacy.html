Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy is provided as a template summary of how the service actually works today. Before relying on it commercially, have it reviewed by legal counsel for your jurisdiction and customer base.

What we collect

  • Account information — your email address, name, optional company name, and a hashed password (we never store your password in plain text).
  • Documents you upload — contracts, amendments, and invoices you submit for analysis.
  • Audit results — the findings, summaries, and advisory text generated for you, stored so you can revisit them in your history.
  • Billing information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number; we keep only your Stripe customer/subscription identifiers and plan status.

How uploaded documents are handled

  • Uploaded files are processed in memory and are not written to disk on our servers.
  • The extracted text of your documents is sent to OpenAI (our AI subprocessor) to perform the analysis. Per OpenAI's API data-usage policy, API data is not used to train their models.
  • The structured results of your audit (not the original files) are stored in our database so they appear in your audit history.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.
  • We do not use your documents to train AI models.
  • We do not store card numbers or payment credentials.

Security

All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Sessions use httpOnly, secure cookies, and only a hash of the session token is stored server-side. Audit results are only accessible from the account that created them.

Data retention & deletion

Audit history is retained while your account is active. To delete your account and all associated audit history, contact us at the email below and we will remove it.
