MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Vendor: CloudOps Software Inc.
Customer: Acme Manufacturing LLC
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Initial Term: 24 months (January 1, 2024 through December 31, 2025)

1. AUTO-RENEWAL
This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month terms unless
either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days
prior to the end of the then-current term.

2. PRICING SCHEDULE
Per-seat license fees shall be billed monthly according to the following
volume tiers, based on the total number of active seats:

  - 1 to 100 seats:    $42.00 per seat per month
  - 101 to 250 seats:  $35.00 per seat per month
  - 251+ seats:        $28.00 per seat per month

3. ANNUAL PRICE ESCALATION CAP
Customer's effective per-seat rate shall not increase by more than three
percent (3%) in any rolling twelve (12) month period, regardless of any
changes to Vendor's list pricing ("Escalator Cap").

4. PREMIUM SUPPORT
Customers maintaining one hundred fifty (150) or more active seats are
entitled to Premium Support at no additional charge for the duration of
this Agreement.

5. ONE-TIME FEES
A one-time Platform Setup & Onboarding Fee of $5,000.00 shall be billed on
Customer's first invoice only, and shall not recur on any subsequent
invoice.

6. LATE PAYMENT
Overdue balances accrue a late fee of 1.5% per month.
