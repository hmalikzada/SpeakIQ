MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING — AMENDMENT NO. 1

Between: CloudOps Software Inc. ("Vendor")
And:     Acme Manufacturing LLC ("Customer")
Effective Date: July 1, 2024

Reference: Master Service Agreement dated January 1, 2024

The parties agree as follows:

1. LOYALTY DISCOUNT
In recognition of Customer's expanded deployment, Vendor shall apply a
five percent (5%) Loyalty Discount to all per-seat license fees on every
invoice issued on or after July 1, 2024, for the remainder of the
Agreement term and any renewal term.

2. ALL OTHER TERMS
All other terms of the Master Service Agreement, including the volume
pricing tiers, the 3% annual escalation cap, and the Premium Support
entitlement, remain unchanged and in full force.
